**Issues? Follow this process:** 

---

## 1. CHECK DELIVERY WINDOW

| Item | Window |
|------|--------|
| Cards | 3 hours |
| Fullz / Drops | 6 hours |
| Custom orders | End of day (10pm CET) |

If within window: **wait**. If past window, proceed to next step.

---

## 2. VERIFICATION ISSUES

**If item doesn't work:**

1. Take **video screen recording** showing the problem
2. Include **order number** in recording
3. DM @thegoldeniceberg or @MRMONKSX with:
   - Order #
   - Issue description
   - Video proof

**No proof = no replacement.**  
**No low risk proxy used = no replacement.**

---

## 3. REPLACEMENT PROCESS

- Approved replacements sent within **24 hours**
- Replacement value = original or better
- **One replacement per order**

---

## 4. REFUNDS

**Refunds only considered if:**

- Item never delivered beyond max window
- Replacement also fails
- Vendor error (proven)

Refunds processed in crypto to original address only.
